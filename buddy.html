What is Buddy?

IoT Smart Baby Monitor

Buddy marks a revolutionary leap forward in baby care, integrating the functions of traditional infant ecosystem devices from the past decade into a single, user-friendly smart device. Harnessing the power of advanced technology and sensors, seamlessly managed via an IoT app, Buddy stands as the pinnacle solution for contemporary families, reshaping the baby care landscape. Integration of Alexa and Google Home capabilities, Buddy elevates convenience and accessibility to unprecedented levels, establishing a fresh benchmark for modern parenting experiences.


Key Features

  • Information source and collection. Focus on overall health, growth monitoring and sleep training utilising IoT capabilities.
  • Personalised automation. Parental assistance, reminders, baby routine establishment and predictive maintenance.
  • Remote Monitoring. A two way audio and wide view high resolution baby monitoring camera.

Prototypes

The Phone App

Wireframing and prototyping apps play a crucial role in developing Buddy's design. They enable us to visualize and fine-tune its features, ensuring intuitive navigation and flawless functionality. Utilizing these tools, we craft interactive prototypes showcasing Buddy's real-time monitoring and user-friendly interface. These prototypes serve as a dynamic platform for investors to experience firsthand how Buddy operates. By leveraging these technologies, we streamline our development process, foster collaboration, and demonstrate Buddy's potential to deliver an unparalleled baby care experience, captivating investor interest.

Responsive Website

Creating a responsive website to complement Buddy's app prototype is a pivotal step in our development process. Just as wireframing and prototyping apps enable us to visualize and refine Buddy's features, a responsive website ensures seamless accessibility across various devices. Through this platform, we showcase Buddy's capabilities and user-friendly interface, allowing investors to experience its full potential firsthand. By leveraging responsive design principles, we not only streamline the development process but also demonstrate Buddy's versatility and commitment to delivering an exceptional baby care experience, captivating investor interest along the way.
